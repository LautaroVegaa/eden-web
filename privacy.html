Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Eden ("Eden", "we", "us") handles information when you use the Eden mobile app. By using Eden, you agree to this policy.

The short version

  • Eden has no accounts and no login.
  • Your name, answers, and prayer history are stored on your device.
  • When you request a prayer or reply, that text is processed by Cloudflare and Anthropic. If you use Listen, the generated text is also processed by OpenAI to create audio.
  • Eden does not persist prayer, chat, or audio content on its servers. We do not sell your data.

Information we process

Stored on your device only

Your name, onboarding answers, chosen reminder time, streak, and your saved prayers and messages are stored locally on your device. We do not have a copy of this on our servers.

Prayer and chat generation

When you request a prayer or a reply in "Pray with Eden", your first name and the text you submit are sent over an encrypted connection to our processing service on Cloudflare and to Anthropic. They process this content only to return the prayer or reply you requested. Eden does not write this content to its server databases or logs.

Listen and audio generation

If you choose Listen, the generated prayer or reply is sent over an encrypted connection through Cloudflare to OpenAI to create audio. The audio is streamed back to your device with caching disabled. Eden does not store the submitted text or generated audio in its server databases.

Security and usage records

To prevent fraud and enforce free-use and daily limits, Eden processes a random RevenueCat app user identifier, an App Attest public-key registration and counter, and limited usage-ledger entries. These records do not contain your prayer or chat content. Daily counters expire automatically after approximately two days. Other records remain until you use the in-app deletion control or they are no longer operationally necessary.

Subscriptions

Purchases are processed by Apple. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives a randomly generated app user identifier and purchase information to tell the app whether your subscription is active. We never receive your full payment details.

Notifications

Daily reminders are scheduled locally on your device. We do not send push notifications from a server.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell or rent your personal information.
  • We do not use your prayer content to train AI models.
  • We do not require an account or collect your contacts.

Third-party services

  • Apple — app distribution and payments.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management.
  • Cloudflare — secure request processing.
  • Anthropic — AI generation of prayers and replies.
  • OpenAI — temporary audio generation when you use Listen.

We limit each provider to the data needed for its stated function and require it to protect that data under applicable service and data-processing terms. Each provider also publishes its own privacy policy.

Children

Eden is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Your choices

  • Delete cloud data: In Eden, open Profile → Privacy & data → Delete cloud data. This deletes Eden's usage ledger and App Attest registration and requests permanent deletion of the associated RevenueCat customer record and purchase history.
  • Delete local data: Deleting the app removes the name, onboarding answers, prayer history, messages, and streak stored on your device.
  • Subscriptions: Cloud-data deletion and app deletion do not cancel an Apple subscription. You can manage or cancel it in your Apple account settings.

If the in-app deletion control is unavailable, email edensupport@gmail.com and include the anonymous Eden identifier shown in the prefilled support message.

Not medical or pastoral advice

Eden provides AI-generated prayers and encouragement. It is not a substitute for professional counseling, medical advice, or pastoral care. If you are struggling with your mental health, please reach out to a licensed professional. If you are in crisis, contact your local emergency services.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
