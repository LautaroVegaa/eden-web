Frequently asked questions

What does Eden do?

Eden writes a short, personal Christian prayer based on how you're feeling, grounded in a Bible verse. You can also pray with Eden in a guided chat and listen to your prayer read aloud.

Is Eden free?

Everyone gets one free personalized prayer. Unlimited prayers, the "Pray with Eden" chat, and Listen (audio) require a subscription.

What do subscriptions cost?

  • Weekly — $6.99/week, with a 3-day free trial.
  • Yearly — $39.99/year.

Both are auto-renewable subscriptions billed through your Apple account. Your subscription renews automatically unless you cancel at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

How do I restore a subscription?

Open Eden → Profile → Subscription → Restore purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the Apple ID you subscribed with.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open Eden → Profile → Subscription → Manage subscription, or go to Settings → your name → Subscriptions on your iPhone. Cancelling stops future renewals; you keep access until the current period ends.

How do I delete my data?

Open Eden → Profile → Privacy & data → Delete cloud data. Deleting the app also removes everything stored on your device. Cancelling or deleting does not cancel an Apple subscription — manage that in your Apple account.

Is Eden a medical or crisis service?

No. Eden offers spiritual support, not medical advice, therapy, or emergency care. If you may harm yourself or need urgent help, contact your local emergency services. In the US, call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline).
